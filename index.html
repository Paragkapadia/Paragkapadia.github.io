Know Me

MY JOURNEY

Hey, Parag Kapadia this side, an explorer of the wild belongs to Vadodara who has been doing Safaris right from the age of 17. For over sevral remarkable years, I've explored untrodden paths, immortalizing the raw beauty that graces our Earth and still getting more ways from the experts. So, I can take it to the next level with my experience and knowledge.

Through the lens, I capture untamed wonders. With each click, I weave tales of nature's secrets, as a wildlife photographer and content creator. From the soaring eagles to the elusive big cats, my camera has whispered stories that words cannot convey. Join me on this visual safari, where every frame is a window into the untamed symphony of life. Step into my world, where passion and pixels collide, and let me transport you to the heart of nature's masterpiece..
